Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long does delivery take?
Typically 5–7 business days. For urgent orders, message us on WhatsApp and we'll do our best!
Can I customize the products?
Absolutely! All our products are fully customizable — names, colors, designs, messages. Just let us know your preferences.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI and bank transfers. Simply place your order on WhatsApp and we'll share the payment details — quick and easy!
Do you ship outside Tamil Nadu?
Yes! We ship across India. For international orders, please contact us on WhatsApp to discuss shipping options.
What if I'm not satisfied?
Your satisfaction is our priority. If anything isn't right, contact us within 48 hours of delivery and we'll make it right.
Can I place bulk / corporate orders?
Yes! We love bulk orders for events, weddings, and corporate gifting. Contact us on WhatsApp for special pricing.
